Dream

Instead of giving my aspirations in life, I'm going to tell you about an actual dream I've had. One dream from when I was young is when I realized I was dreaming tried to wake up, could feel my eyelids trying to open but couldn't, started freaking out, woke up into another dream, realized I was dreaming again, and finally woke up. Kinda upsetting that the only lucid dream I've had I immediately tried to wake up from instead of have fun with it, but I was young at the time so it's whatever.
